What services do you provide?

Visux offers a variety of design services - web design, mobile app development, digital marketing, SEO optimization, content creation, social media management, branding and much more. What does your process look like?

First, we 'll invite you to an early discovery meeting to discuss your project and ask you to complete a brief for assessing your needs. Based on this, we 'll craft a proposal with a cost/duration estimate. We also have a special fixed-price package for startups in our offer. Upon agreement, we 'll establish daily communication via Slack and provide ongoing feedback. Our selected designer(s) will work iteratively, updating you on progress through daily stand-up sessions or recorded demonstrations for asynchronous work. You 'll have access to Figma for feedback, and upon project completion, we 'll hand off the project to you. For a detailed understanding of our processes, we 're happy to discuss them further in a call.

How much does it cost?

Our standard rate is $75 USD per hour. Typically, we operate on a time and materials basis or offer retainers. A retainer involves a recurring fee, enabling you to secure our services continuously. This is particularly beneficial if you have various design-related tasks spread across multiple projects. Feel free to inquire for more details.
